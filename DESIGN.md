# Design System: A11y & Plain Language Toolkit

## Core Principles
- **Accessibility First**: Every component must meet WCAG 2.2 Level AA/AAA standards.
- **Reader-Centric**: Information is processed for readability, not just aesthetics.
- **Plain Language**: Targeted at a 6th-8th grade reading level.
- **Easy Read**: Targeted at a 3rd-5th grade reading level with high visual structure.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Open Sans (Sans-serif)
- **Body Size**: 16px (1rem)
- **Line Height**: 1.5
- **Easy Read Size**: 20px (1.25rem)
- **Easy Read Line Height**: 1.8

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: #1A1C1E (Solid Navy/Black)
- **Background**: #F7F5F2 (Warm Grey)
- **Dark Mode Background**: #0f1113
- **Contrast**: High contrast (meeting 7:1 ratio for critical text).

## Layout
- **Container**: Max-width 6xl (1152px)
- **Spacing**: Generous padding (p-6, p-8) to ensure focus.
- **Components**: Rounded corners (rounded-xl) for a approachable feel.

## Visual Anchors
- Use of icons (lucide-react) and emojis as signposts.
- Limited paragraph length (max 5 lines for Easy Read).
